Give your child the academic advantage to compete in today's world

Kumon helps accelerate your child’s learning — from as young as age 3 all the way through high school

The proven Kumon Method can help your child build a lifetime of math and reading success.

Short, incremental assignments help students build on the skills they learn.

Developed by Toru Kumon, the Kumon Method allows students to work independently and at their own pace.

The goal is to develop your child's critical and self-learning skills. Your instructor will develop a unique lesson plan customized to your child's ability. This ensures that there is no gap in their learning.

Carefully designed worksheets

Kumon Worksheets cover everything from counting to calculus, and phonemics to Shakespeare. We continually fine-tune worksheets to keep them relevant and effective in today's world.

Advancing step-by-step

Each worksheet is one step more advanced than the one before, ensuring your child always has a grasp of the fundamental learning before moving on through the curriculum. We won't progress your child to the next step if they're not ready yet.

Learning that is truly absorbed

Correcting errors is very important in Kumon. We want your child to be able to identify their mistakes because that helps to contribute to how well they will master the concept.

Examples that demonstrate

The worksheets provide examples that demonstrate how to solve each type of problem, empowering your child to self-learn new concepts.

How does Kumon work day-to-day?

Your child's daily assignment take about 30 minutes per subject to complete.

Two days a week your child completes assignments at a Kumon centre; the other five assignments are completed at home.
